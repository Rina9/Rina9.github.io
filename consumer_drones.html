Consumer drones are normally priced from $200 upwards. However as more drones become available (due to competition on the market)the price is expected to drop and a wider variety of drones would be accessible on the market. These aerial vehicles are not to be used for military work as they are not suitable for conflict zones.These kinds of drones can be used to:

Take beautiful pictures

Walk the dog

Watch the house

The pros are that you'll have more free time and you'd be less stressed.

The cons are that the drone could get out of control and lose the dog. Also it is not guaranteed to do what it is programmed all the time
