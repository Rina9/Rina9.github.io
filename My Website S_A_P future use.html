Opportunities for Drones in the Future

Drones will make it easier for us human beings in the future. It can be used to deliver items such as groceries, fast food, beauty products,etc. They can also be used to create cinematic views for movies or used to survey the community instead of having a neighbourhood watch. They could be used as speakers to advertise or broadcast news.

Challenges for Drones in the Future

For everything good there is something bad. Meaning that not everyone will like the concept of having drones. Some may protest this rapid development in technology. Certain items needed to build particular drones might be in limited supply so the drones themselves might be sold at a higher price. Lastly, the drones can be a danger to animals,meaning they could harm a bird with its blades.
